A NEW COOPERATIVE
AND SOLO EXPERIENCE

Uncharted 4: Survival is a new wave-based mode where players can take on
swarms of increasingly powerful enemies, unique objectives, and intense
boss battles. Take on the challenge solo or work together with up to 2
additional friends to overcome 50 waves across a variety of maps to rank up,
expand their arsenal, and earn rewards. Best of all, Survival is available as a
downloadable update for all Uncharted 4 owners at no additional cost.

OVERCOME UNIQUE
CHALLENGE

Survival is all about strategy, camaraderie, and skill. Alongside intense
firefights, Survival features a wide array of objective modes and
modifiers that will introduce new challenges as players progress
through Waves. To reach Wave 50, you’ll need to defend key areas of
the map in Siege, scavenge for pickups in Treasure Hunt, or take out
VIPs in Marked Man. As time progresses, enemies will also become
more precise, more aggressive, and better equipped. Those looking for
a challenge can choose from a variety of difficulty levels, including
Crushing Mode.

TAKE ON THE
WARLORD OF SURVIVAL

Every 10 waves of Survival will culminate in an intense boss battle. From
heavily armored Brutes to the undead Pirate Lords of Libertalia, these
powerful Warlords will require skill and carefully measured tactics to
overcome. They not only wield considerable firepower, many are equipped
with Mystical abilities adding dynamic new gameplay as you progress.
